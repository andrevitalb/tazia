About Us

TAZIA was born in Puebla, Mexico, as a creative platform. Over time, we found that we loved to experiment with fabrics, y rescue materials to practice conscious fashion.

Today we are a team of 8 people who design, draw, search, cut, sew, publish, revise and sell jackets.

Our beginnings started in 2016 experimenting with vintage designs and urban fashion , exploring textiles, styles and proposals.

Little by little we find a taste for jackets, ponchos and coats for people with the same interest in trying new things.

Since then, we connect with people who want to wear something that identifies them and contributes to their individuality.

Limited Edition | Sustainable fashion

We belive in Sustainable fashion, fair payment, customized attention and Mexican design.
Since August 2020, we have been part of the Noissue Eco-Packaging Alliance, so all your orders will be wrapped in recycled paper and shipped in compostable bags, which biodegrade in just 180 days, or in recycled boxes and salvaged for a second use.

75% of our collections use salvaged or waste materials, following upcycling / supra-recycling practices. Therefore, the number of pieces that we can make of a design depends on the existence of the fabrics.

Our articles are produced after you finalize your purchase. For this reason, shipments will take 2 to 7 business days to complete. We will communicate with you at all times to keep you informed of the status of your garment.

If you have any specific instructions, or any questions or special requests, please do not hesitate to contact us.

Thank you!
